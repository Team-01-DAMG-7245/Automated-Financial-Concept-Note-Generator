vector

Estimates for value-at-risk of portfolio returns for each portfolio in pwgt, returned as a NumPorts

More About

Value-at-Risk

Value-at-Risk (VaR) estimates the maximum potential loss that a portfolio could face over a specified
time period, given normal market conditions and a specified confidence level.

Tips

You can also use dot notation to estimate the value-at-risk of PortfolioCVaR object.

pvar = obj.estimatePortVaR(pwgt);
