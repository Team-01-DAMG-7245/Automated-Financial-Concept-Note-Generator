Plot the qx series and display the legend. The series qx is the conditional probability that a person at
age x will die between age x and the next age in the series

plot((0:100), log(qx));
legend(series, 'location', 'southeast');
title('Conditional Probability of Dying within One Year of Current Age');
xlabel('Age');
ylabel('Log Probability');
