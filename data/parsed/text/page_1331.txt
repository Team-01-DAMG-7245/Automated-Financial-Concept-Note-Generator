Compute Total Performance Attribution

Use the brinsonAttribution object with totalAttribution to compute the total performance
attribution of the portfolio summed up for all categories.

[AggregateTotalAttribution, PeriodicTotalAttribution] = totalAttribution(BrinsonPAobj)
