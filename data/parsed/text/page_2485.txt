medprice

Median price from the series of high and low prices

Syntax

MedianPrice = medprice(Data)

Description

MedianPrice = medprice(Data) calculates the median prices from the series of high and low
prices. The median price is the average of the high and low prices for each period.

Examples

Calculate the Median Prices for a Data Series for a Stock

Load the file SimulatedStock.mat, which provides a timetable (TMW) for financial data for TMW
stock.

load SimulatedStock.mat
MedianPrice = medprice(TMW);
plot(MedianPrice.Time,MedianPrice.MedianPrice) 
title('Median Price for TMW')
