lx — Collection of num discrete survival counts
matrix

Collection of num discrete survival counts, specified as an N-by-num matrix. The input lx series is the
number of people alive at age x, given 100,000 alive at birth. Values of 0 or NaN in the input table lx
are ignored.

Data Types: double
