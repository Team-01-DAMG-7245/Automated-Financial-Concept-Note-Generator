Compute Category Weights

Use categoryWeights to compute the average and periodic category weights for the portfolio and
the benchmark, as well as, the corresponding active weights.

[AverageCategoryWeights,PeriodicCategoryWeights] = categoryWeights(BrinsonPAobj)
